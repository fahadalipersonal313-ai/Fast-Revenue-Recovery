FAQ

Questions you'd actually ask.

Does it send messages on my behalf?

No. Ever. The app drafts messages and saves them to your Gmail Drafts folder. You open your inbox, review, and hit send yourself. Every customer interaction comes from you, not from us.

Do I need Xero or QuickBooks?

No. Most tools in this space require an accounting integration — which means more setup and a higher price tag. We work directly from any Excel or CSV spreadsheet.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. Each account gets a fully isolated encrypted database. Your email password is encrypted with Fernet using a server-side secret. No one else — including other users of the app — can read your data.

What email providers do you support?

Today: Gmail and Google Workspace (via an App Password — takes 2 minutes to set up). Microsoft 365 / Outlook OAuth integration is on the roadmap.

Will it stay free?

Early access is free, with no time limit for users who sign up now. When paid tiers launch later, current users will be grandfathered in at no cost.
